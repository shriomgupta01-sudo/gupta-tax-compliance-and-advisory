Frequently Asked

Before you reach out

Quick answers to the questions clients ask most before their first consultation.

What documents should I bring for an income tax filing?

PAN and Aadhaar, Form 16 (if salaried), bank statements for the financial year, details of any other income (rent, capital gains, interest), and proof of deductions you plan to claim (80C investments, insurance, home loan interest, etc.). If you're unsure what applies to you, bring what you have — the rest can be worked out together.

Do you work with clients outside Dombivli?

Yes. Most compliance work — return filing, GST filings, advisory calls — is handled remotely over phone, email and video. In-person meetings are available for clients in and around Dombivli, Maharashtra.

How is your fee structured?

Fees depend on the complexity and scope of the engagement — a straightforward salaried ITR is priced differently from ongoing GST compliance or business advisory. Reach out with your specific situation and you'll get a clear quote before any work begins.

I've received a tax notice — can you help?

Yes — notice review, response drafting and representation before tax and GST authorities are part of the practice. Share the notice as early as possible so there's enough time to respond before the deadline.
